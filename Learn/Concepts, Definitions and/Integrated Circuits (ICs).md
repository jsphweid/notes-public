# Integrated Circuits (ICs)

Not necessarily what you'd think. I used to think Integrated Chip could be used interchangeably with circuit/chip/board/etc. but that's not completely the case

A circuit is usually composed of a bunch of tiny parts like transistors, resistors, diodes, etc. An integrated circuit is simply a specific configuration of a *ton* of those in as tiny space as possible basically.

A CPU is an IC, but not all ICs are CPUs.
